QUARTZITE

Quartzite is a metamorphic rock that has been formed from sandstone. In its purest form itâ€™s white like marble but it can have impurities that lead to some incredible patterns and colors.

Quartzite is an extremely hard rock that is harder than granite, so it takes heat very well. While some quartzites are very dense and resistant to water and acids, others are porous and not as durable. Application of sealer helps to protect the surface against etching and staining.


Recommended use


This attractive looking stone is suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. It is suitable for interior and exterior walls, spas and showers, bathroom vanities, fireplace surrounds, flooring and kitchen countertops. Available in polished, honed and brushed finishes.
