QUARTZITE

Quartzite is a metamorphic rock, it consists mainly from quartz (more than 80%) and it can be found in massive blocks or stratified. This attractive stone is suitable for both indoor and outdoor areas.

Quartzite is harder than granite, so it is extremely durable and takes heat very well. Being a very chemically resistant stone, its surface can however be scratched by sharp objects. It also shares the weakness of all natural stones stain.


Recommended use


Due to its highly reactive nature to acids it is not recommended for kitchen countertops, it is more commonly used for flooring, vanities, sinks and fireplace surrounds. Because of its beauty and softness it has always been used by artists
