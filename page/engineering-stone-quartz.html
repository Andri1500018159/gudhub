ENGINEERED STONE/QUARTZ

Engineered stone is a material made of crushed natural stone and a polymer resin binder. Quartz is the key ingredient (usually about 93%). This combination is used to enhance properties of natural stone, and form it into a product that is more workable and usable.

Engineered stone is one of the most environmentally friendly materials you can choose. It is less porous, more flexible, and harder than many types of natural stone. One of the most practical options of engineered stone is that it stands up to staining, heat and scratching. Unlike natural stone, it never requires sealing.


Recommended use


Listed above benefits make engineered stone a perfect choice for countertops. In addition to kitchen countertops, engineered stone products are suitable for shower and tub surrounds, vanity tops and other surfaces in wet areas.The cons are that comparing to natural stone this material can be really expensive and although it can come pretty close to looking like real stone it is still not such and does not have the same natural color variations.
