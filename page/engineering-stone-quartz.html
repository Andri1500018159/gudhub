ENGINEERED STONE/QUARTZ

Engineered stone is a compound made of crushed natural quartz (usually about 93%) combined with highquality polyester resins and pigments. It is available in a wide spectrum of colors and finishes.

Engineered stone is one of the most environmentally friendly materials. It is less porous and harder than most natural stone options. Quartz countertops are very durable, scratch and stain resistant. Not affected by heat or household chemicals and acids. Quartz surfaces also offer a bacteriostatic protection.


Recommended use


The benefits listed above make engineered stone a perfect choice for countertops. In addition to kitchen countertops, quartz products are suitable for shower and tub surrounds, vanity tops and more. Engineered stone is virtually maintenance free.
