GRANITE

Granite is one of the oldest, most popular and respected natural stones on the market. Available in striking array of colors, granite's durability and longevity makes it ideal for kitchen countertops and other heavily used surfaces, including table tops and floors.

Granite is highly resistant to scratching as it is one of the hardest stone materials. It is heat resistant and is not typically affected by citric acid, coffee, tea, alcohol or wine. With proper sealing and cleaning, granite will not stain under normal use.


Recommended use


Granite received the the highest overall performance rating as as kitchen countertop material. Because of its exceptional strength, granite is well suited for interior flooring and exterior applications such as cladding, paving and curbing.
