ONYX/SEMI-PRECIOUS STONE

The term semi-precious is used to refer to the entire world of colored gemstones, not including precious gems like diamond, ruby, sapphire and emerald. Onyx is one of the most rare semi-natural stones in the world. It can be found in a wide range of colors, often layered.

Due to its spectacular beauty and translucency onyx is a favorite accent stone in interior design. However the biggest problem with onyx is that it is very soft and does not hold up well with regular use at home. It can easily be scratched, acidic liquids like juices and alcohol are danger for it.


Recommended use


Semi-precious stones can be implemented in flooring, giving a luxurious look to your floor. Various types of sinks, vanity tops, bathtubs, claddings are made by these stones. Onyx as material is rarely chosen for countertops, although it is the most expensive material you can get it is actually more decorative than practical.
