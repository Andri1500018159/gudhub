ONYX/SEMI-PRECIOUS STONE

The term semi-precious is used to refer to the entire world of colored gemstones, not including precious gems like diamond, ruby, sapphire and emerald. Onyx is one of the most rare semi-natural stones in the world. It can be found in a wide range of colors, often layered.

Onyx is a calcareous stone and prone to etching and staining from acids such as ketchup, lemons, alcohol, and household cleaners. Great care is needed to maintain the natural beauty of this stone. Similar to limestone, onyx is a softer stone best placed where it won't be used or abused on a day to day basis. It is porous and needs to be sealed


Recommended use


Onyx can be implemented in areas where you can accent the stone and use under lighting or backlighting to draw attention to its translucent qualities. It is most often used as fireplace surround, vanity, bar top or wall elevation. Other uses include light duty residential floors, sinks, table tops and novelty items such as vases and lamps.
