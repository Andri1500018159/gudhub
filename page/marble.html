MARBLE

Marble is a metamorphic rock formed when a limestone or dolomite is exposed to the right temperature or pressure. During this process, other minerals are introduced into the structure which produce beautiful colors and veining that characterize marble.

Marble adds a sophisticated element to your home. It is available in a beautiful spectrum of colors. However it is sensitive to acidic substances such as vinegar, juice, wine and coffee. Following a few care and maintenance tips will preserve marbleâ€™s natural beauty and elegance


Recommended use


Marble is not scratch and stain resistant, so it’s not recommended for heavily used areas as kitchen countertops. Marble is most commonly used for flooring, vanities, fireplace surrounds and interior wall elevations.
